## Methodology Notes

### Profile Structure

Each profile follows this format:
1. **Header** - Name, title
2. **Sources** - Primary texts consulted
3. **Tags** - Cross-reference categories
4. **Overview** - Summary paragraph
5. **Key Information** - Core facts and details
6. **Quotes** - Direct citations with sources
7. **Contradictions & Notes** - Ambiguities and interpretations
8. **Further Research Needed** - Identified gaps
9. **Cross-References** - Related profiles

### Source Priority

Profiles prioritize:
1. Direct statements from sourcebooks
2. Information provided by reliable narrators
3. Game statistics and mechanics
4. Contextual inference

### Quotes and Citations

All direct quotes include source attribution. Page numbers are included where visible in the text extracts.
