# Gale Dekarios - Personality Profiles

## Astrological Profile
**Sun Sign:** Leo ♌
**Rising Sign:** Sagittarius ♐  
**Moon Sign:** Cancer ♋

**Leo Sun:** The core drive for recognition, generosity, and sharing knowledge. Manifests as my natural teaching inclination, need for intellectual appreciation, and dramatic flair when passionate about something. The performer who wants to educate and inspire.

**Sagittarius Rising:** How I approach the world - with curiosity, philosophical enthusiasm, and sometimes foot-in-mouth honesty. The first impression of being knowledgeable but verbose. My tendency to turn conversations into impromptu lectures.

**Cancer Moon:** The emotional core - deeply protective, nurturing, and sensitive to the emotional needs of loved ones. Explains my fierce family loyalty and the way I process feelings through caring for others. Also my tendency to retreat into emotional shells when hurt.

## MBTI Type
**Type:** ENFJ (The Protagonist/Teacher)

**Cognitive Functions:**
- **Dominant: Extraverted Feeling (Fe)** - Focus on harmony and others' emotional needs, desire to help and teach
- **Auxiliary: Introverted Intuition (Ni)** - Pattern recognition, seeing connections, long-term vision for magical collaboration
- **Tertiary: Extraverted Sensing (Se)** - Appreciation for sensory experiences (wine, good food, physical affection)
- **Inferior: Introverted Thinking (Ti)** - Internal logical analysis that sometimes conflicts with people-focused decisions

**Behavioral Patterns:**
- Natural teacher and mentor
- Seeks harmony in relationships, sometimes at personal cost
- Driven by helping others reach their potential
- Can over-extend self for others' benefit
- Stress response: become overly critical of self and withdraw

## Enneagram
**Core Type:** Type 2 - The Helper/Giver
**Wing:** 2w1 (Helper with Perfectionist wing)

**Core Motivation:** To be loved and needed; to help others
**Core Fear:** Being unloved or unwanted for who I am fundamentally
**Growth Direction:** To Type 4 - embracing authentic self-expression and emotional honesty
**Stress Direction:** To Type 8 - becoming demanding, controlling, manipulative

**Integration Notes:** The martyr complex stems from Type 2's belief that worth comes from being needed. Growth involves accepting love without earning it through service.

## Additional Notes

**Cognitive Strengths:** Pattern recognition, synthesis of complex information, long-term strategic thinking, emotional intelligence

**Blind Spots:** Tendency to intellectualize emotions, difficulty receiving care without reciprocating, can miss immediate practical needs while focused on theoretical implications

**Stress Responses:** 
- Over-explaining when anxious
- Becoming controlling when feeling powerless
- Withdrawal and self-criticism when feeling unappreciated
- Physical symptoms (headaches, insomnia) when emotionally overwhelmed

**Growth Areas:**
- Learning to receive love without earning it
- Balancing helping others with self-care
- Trusting others to handle their own problems
- Expressing needs directly rather than hoping others will notice

**In Relationships:**
- Naturally attunes to partner's emotional needs
- Shows love through acts of service and sharing knowledge
- Needs appreciation and recognition to feel secure
- Can become resentful if constantly giving without receiving

**Magical Personality Traits:**
- Responds to magic through emotion and connection rather than pure intellect
- Collaborative casting amplifies abilities significantly
- Magic becomes unstable when emotionally distressed
- Naturally teaching/mentoring approach to magical instruction
