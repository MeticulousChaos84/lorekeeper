# Grove - Squirrel Encounter

**Source:** `DEN_General_Squirrel.html`
**Starting Node:** Various

---

The player encounters a friendly squirrel in the Druid Grove. If they kick it to death, companions react with varying degrees of horror and sarcasm.

## Companion Reactions to Squirrel Murder

**Astarion:** "Do you always take on such terrifying foes?"

**Gale:** "Surely you should be reserving your foot for more deserving foes? That poor creature was harmless!"

**Shadowheart:** "How heroic. I was wondering if we'd survive encountering such a fearsome beast."

**Wyll:** "Gods! What in the deepest Hell is wrong with you?"

**Karlach:** "What. The. Hells."

## Player Responses

If the player claims "It came right at me!":

**Approval:** Astarion approves (+1). Wyll, Shadowheart, and Karlach all disapprove (-1).

Other responses include:
- "It's horrible! I'm horrible!"
- "My body moved without my command... I couldn't control it."
- *Keep quiet.*
