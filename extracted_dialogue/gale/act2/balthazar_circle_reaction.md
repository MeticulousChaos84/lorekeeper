# Balthazar's Circle - Gale's Reaction
*Act 2 - Moonrise Towers, Balthazar's Laboratory*

**Context:** If the player successfully uses Balthazar's necromantic circle without Gale's help.

---

## If Circle Used Successfully (Sorcerer)

**Gale:** A fine job you did back there. Not *exactly* how I would have approached it, but we all have our quirks when it comes to using magic. Some more than others.

Now, given my magical expertise is of limited use to you, was there something else you wanted to ask me about?

---

## If Player Did It Without Gale's Help (General)

**Gale:** There's the makings of a fine follower of Mystra within you, give or take a few decades of studying the Weave.

Now, given my magical expertise is of limited use to you, was there something else you wanted to ask me about?

---

## If Circle Exploded

**Gale:** Nothing like a good singeing to teach us the perils of dabbling with magic we don't understand. And the wisdom of stepping aside for those who do.

So - assuming you're more willing to take my words to heart from now on - what can I do for you?

---

## If Gale Crafted the Dark Lantern Instead

**Gale:** If you'd prefer to rely on beginner's luck than my myriad experiences in studying such magics, who am I to judge?

Now, given my magical expertise is of limited use to you, was there something else you wanted to ask me about?
