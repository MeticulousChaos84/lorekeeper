# Gale Origin: Death Protocol - The Pouch

**Context:** Interaction with the sealed magical pouch found on Gale's body after death. Opening it incorrectly destroys the contents; opening it correctly reveals a letter and flute needed for resurrection.

**Source:** `ORI_Gale_DeathPouch.html`

---

## Examining the Pouch

**Narrator:** *The pouch is sealed with differently coloured strands of thread.*

### If Instructions Were Given

**Narrator:** *An inscription on the leather reads: 'Do not open unless I have instructed you to do so. In case of doubt, await instructions near my body. Gale, Wizard of Waterdeep.'*

---

## Opening the Pouch Correctly

*Pull the purple thread in a counter-clockwise fashion.*

**Narrator:** *As the purple thread becomes undone, the pouch opens to reveal a letter and flute inside.*

---

## Opening the Pouch Incorrectly

*Pull the threads to open the pouch.*

**Narrator:** *You get a feeling something's wrong. It's as if the pouch struggles against you.*

### If You Keep Pulling

*You keep pulling.*

**Narrator:** *As the threads become undone, the pouch itself evaporates.*

**Narrator (if protocol known):** *The letter and flute inside are now forever beyond your grasp.*

**Narrator (if protocol unknown):** *Whatever contents were inside, they've been utterly destroyed.*

---

## Other Options

*You leave the pouch alone.*

*You put the pouch away.*
