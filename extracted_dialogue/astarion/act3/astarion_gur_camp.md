## The Confrontation

**Ulma:** The last time your friend came to our camp, he stole our children. Our future.

**Gandrel:** *(if alive)* When I was hunting you, I was to bring you back here. To interrogate you, discover how to save our children, and then destroy you.

**Ulma:** *(if Gandrel is dead)* When we sent Gandrel after you, we wanted to interrogate you. To discover how to save our children and then destroy you. But things have changed. You have changed. Is it true you left your master? That you broke the spell that binds you to him?

**Astarion:** Well, I mean... kind of? It's a long story, honestly.

**Ulma:** Free? Not while his master still lives. But he has, perhaps, earned a second chance.

*Or:*

**Ulma:** One is just as important as the other, I find. And our luck has been poor.

*Or:*

**Ulma:** For those your friend stole from us, it is a matter of life and death.

---

## The Request

**Ulma:** We have tried to save our children once already, attacking Cazador Szarr's palace at first light. Even then, it was too well defended. But if his own spawn approached? Someone he thought he could control? He would throw his doors open and welcome you in. And once inside, you could do what we could not. You could save the children you damned.

**Astarion:** You don't know Cazador like I do - he's merciless. You want me to march into the lion's den and save your children, but I promise you, they're already dead.

### Path: Accepting the Quest

**Astarion:** Excuse me? Did you not hear anything I just said? They're gone.

*Or:*

**Astarion:** I spent two hundred years bringing him victims. Each and every one was whisked away to be fed on that night.

**Ulma:** But you never saw him feed yourself? He could keep prisoners for days before killing them.

**Gandrel:** *(if alive)* I know our plight is grim, but if there is even a chance to save them, we must take it.

**Ulma:** If our children are truly gone, then we ask for blood. I know you can understand that, spawn.

**Astarion:** Gods damn it - fine! If nothing else, at least killing Cazador will feel good.

*Or:*

**Astarion:** I suppose... Yes. Yes, revenge I can do.

---

## Ulma's Benediction

**Ulma:** Thank you, from me and all my people. If you can do this, we will be in your debt. You have lived a life of violence and sin. You have stolen lives, broken families, and caused immeasurable grief. Doing this will not right those wrongs.

**Astarion:** If you're trying to encourage me, you're failing abysmally.

**Ulma:** But it will be a start. You may still be redeemed. Please, go. Time is short, but we will see you again when it is done.

**Astarion:** You do not decide where I should be. Not now, not ever. And you - you've been making friends behind my back. What exactly have you signed us up to?

**Ulma:** You pledged to help us. Have you so quickly gone back on your word?

**Ulma:** Thank you, now please go. We have lost enough time already.
