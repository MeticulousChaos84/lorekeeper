# Cazador's Palace: Ritual Room - Pre-Combat Confrontation

**Location:** Cazador's Palace, Ritual Room
**Context:** Cazador greets Astarion in the ritual room. The other six vampire spawn are held in spectral chains around the room, prepared for the Rite of Profane Ascension. Astarion confronts his former master, who plans to sacrifice him and the others to ascend.

---

## The Confrontation

**Cazador Szarr:** Who stands before us? Is this truly our prodigal son? Do not slouch before me, boy! Have you no respect for yourself? Look at you, crawling back after abandoning your family. You should be begging our forgiveness.

**Astarion:** Forgiveness? You've never forgiven anything. Every mistake, every slip was punished!

**Cazador Szarr:** I strove for perfection in all things - even those as imperfect as you. A pity you amounted to so little, despite my efforts.

**Astarion:** No! No, fuck you and fuck everything you've ever done to me.

**Cazador Szarr:** Oh thankless child. Did I not bless you with our immortal gift? Did I not make you what you are?

**Astarion:** You son of a bitch!

**Cazador Szarr:** You truly forgot my power. You truly thought our bond as creator and creation was all that stopped you from killing me. You are weak, my child. You are a small, pathetic little boy who never amounted to anything.

**Astarion:** Urgh... argh...

**Cazador Szarr:** But today, you will finally do something worthwhile. You will burn, and I will ascend.

*[Cazador attempts to telekinetically hurl Astarion into his place for the ritual. Astarion can attempt to resist.]*

## The Ritual Begins

*[Cazador raises his staff and slams the butt of it against the floor.]*

**Narrator:** *You can feel tendrils of magic snaking through your veins, locking you in place.*

*[Astarion's body jolts and shudders. He spasms, head tilted back, and is locked in place.]*

**Cazador Szarr:** All those souls you brought me, all linked through those lines I carved on your back. All linked through your blood.

### Resisting the Blood Magic

*[Without allies, Astarion must resist alone (DC 10). With allies present, he can force his body to resist (DC 18).]*

#### Success

**Narrator:** *Your body locks, but it moves no further. You can feel Cazador trying to control you, but you fight the magic to a standstill.*

**Cazador Szarr:** Even now you resist? Very well, but you will not stop my ascent. *Ecce dominus!*

*[Combat begins]*

#### Failure

**Narrator:** *You try to resist, but there's nothing you can do. Your body is riddled with the magic - you are his to control. The scars on your back flare to life, as if you can feel the knife in your flesh all over again.*

*[With a twist of his staff, Cazador hurls Astarion through the air, locking him in his spot for the ritual.]*

**Cazador Szarr:** Now we will begin. Now you will witness the birth of the Vampire Ascendant. *Ecce dominus!*

*[Combat begins with Astarion trapped in ritual chains]*

## Notes

- The ritual requires seven vampire spawn, each of whom brought Cazador 1,000 souls (7,000 total)
- The scars on the spawn's backs link them to their victims through blood magic
- Cazador uses this connection to control Astarion
- "Ecce dominus" - Latin for "Behold the master"
- If Astarion fails to resist, he is trapped in spectral chains and must be freed during combat
- The "Vampire Ascendant" would gain the ability to walk in sunlight, freedom from blood hunger, and immense power
