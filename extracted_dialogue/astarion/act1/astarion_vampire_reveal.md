# Astarion's Vampire Reveal
**Location:** In the world (outside camp)
**Act:** 1
**Participants:** Astarion, Player
**Triggers:** Speaking to Astarion after visiting the Creche or entering the Underdark; vampire nature not yet known

---

**Astarion:** Nothing big or terrible, just a small little detail about me that hasn't come up naturally.

**Astarion:** A vampire?

**Astarion:** And by rights I should be cinders blowing in the wind, but something's protecting me. The same something that's kept us from turning into mind flayers, no doubt.

**Astarion:** But that's neither here nor there. What matters is everything's out in the open now. Because we trust each other. *(He doesn't really believe what he's saying but trying to convince the player.)*

**Astarion:** Exactly! I knew I could rely on you. Just like you can rely on me. *(Do not rely on Astarion, dear player. He will eat you.)*
